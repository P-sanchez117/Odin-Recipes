Ingredients

  • 4 large apples
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 tsp of sea salt

Instructions

  1. Core the apples and cut them into wedges
  2. in a saucepan, combine sugar and water over medium heat, stirring until the sugar is dissolved. Continue to cook without stirring until the mixture becomes a golden caramel color
  3. Remove the saucepan from the heat and carefully stir in the heavy cream, salted butter, and sea salt. The mixture will bubble so be cautious
  4. return the sauce pan to low heat and cook, stirring constantly, until the caramel is smooth and all ingredients are fully incorporated.
  5. allow the caramel to cool slightly, and drizzle it over the wedges or serve as a dipping sauce
