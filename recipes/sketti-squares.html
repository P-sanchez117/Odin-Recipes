Sketti Squares


Sketti Squares is a dish that is a cross between Lasagna, and pizza while using spaghetti as a base.


Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Pepperoni
  • Mozzerella Cheese
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1 egg

instructions


  1. Boil a pot of water
  2. Add Spaghetti to the pot once the water is boiling, let the noodles cook for about 3 minutes, or until the pasta is soft
  3. strain the pasta
  4. pre-heat oven to 350 degrees
  5. add the pasta back into the pot or a mixing bowl
  6. add 1 egg and a 1/2 cup of milk to the pasta and mix together
  7. add the pasta to a baking pan [insert pan size here]
  8. add tomato sauce on top of the pasta and spread evenly
  9. add Pepperoni and over the tomato sauce, covering the whole pan
  10. add Mozzerella Cheese on top
  11. add the pan to the oven and bake for 35 minutes, or until the cheese is a nice golden brown on the edge of the dish
  12. once the dish is pulled out of the oven, let it sit for 5 minutes before cutting
